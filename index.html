Moong Dal Dosa
1 cup moong dal, 3/4 inch ginger, 1to2 freen chilies, 2 dried red chilies, 3/4 teaspoon cumin, 1/3 teaspoon salt, 1 cup water, 2 tablespoon ghee or oil to cook.
Preparetion
1.Rinse moong dal thrice and drain the water.Soak them for 15 to 20 mins.
2.Add them to a grinder jar along with salt,ginger,green chilies,red chilies,cumin,and 1 cup water.
3.Grind to a smooth thick, poure more water and blend.
How to Make Moong Dal Dosa
*If using cast iron pan,heat it well.once hot drizzel a few drops of oil and spread it well. the sprinkle little water and turn down the heat to low.
*Pour a ladle full of batter to the center of the pan.Spread the batter in circular pattern to make dosa.
*Sprinkle little ghee towords the edges.let cook on a mediumhigh heat until golden.
*Serve moong dal dosa hot with your favorite chutney.
Idli
1 cup urad dal, 2 cup idli rice,2 tablespoon thick poha, 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds, 3/4 cup chilled water, 3/4 cup water.
Preparetion
1.Add urad dal &methi seed to a bowl. Add idli rice to another bowl.
2.Wah and soak them separately in lot of water at least 6hrs.
3.Rinse and soak poha with 1/4 cup water for about 30 min before blending.
How to Make Idli
*After 6 hrs, drain the water from both the bowls.and add dal, methi, poha, salt, water to blender jar or wet grinder.
*Add rice to blender with water.and transfer this to the urad dal batter and mix well.
*set the batter aside in a warm place for at least 8 to 12hrs.then make idli on a high flame,bring water to boil in an idli steamer.and fill the molds with batter.
*Cover and steam for exactly 10 min on high flame.
*Serve idli hot with sambar & chutney.
Medu Vada
1 cup urad dal, 1/3 teaspoon salt, oil, 2 to 3 green chilies, 1teaspoon ginger garlic pest, ice cold water.
Preparetion
1.Wash dal and soak in water for at least 4 to 5 hrs.
2.Drain water completely and transfer dal to grinder jar,and saltthen start grinding.
3.Whenever needed sprinkle only as much as 1 1/2 tsps, ice cold water evenly.again continue to grind to a coarse thick and fluffy batter.
How to Make Medu Vada
*Heat oil in a deep heavy bottom kadai on a medium heat till hot enough.
*Take small portion of batter. shape to a ball,slightly flatten with thumb.
*Dip the thumb in water,make a hole in the center.gently slide the vada in the hot oil.
*Fry medu vada till they turn golden on both side and regulate flame to medium.
*Serve Medu vada with coconut chutney,or sambar.

Kanda Poha
1.5 cup poha, 1 tsp sugar, ¾ tsp salt, ¼ tsp turmeric, 2 tbsp oil,2 tbsp peanut,2 chilli, 1 onion,¼ tsp turmeric, ¼ tsp salt, 2 tbsp coriander,
Preparetion
1.Take poha in a large strainer.Wash it 1-2 times by gently swifting them with your fingers under running water.
2.Drain the excess water through a strainer. Sprinkle salt and sugar over it, mix/toss well and keep aside.
3.Add green chilies, curry leaves, peanuts and Add chopped onions,Add chopped potatoes and salt.
How to Make Kanda Poha
*Heat 2-tablespoons oil in a pan.and add the Onion,potato,Curry Leaves, Green Chili, peanuts and mix well.
*potatoes turn tender. This will take around 3-4 minutes.Add turmeric powder.Mix well and cook for a minute.
*Add soaked poha and Mix well. Cook for 2-3 minutes. Add lemon juice,and finely chopped coriander leaves.
*Mix well and turn off the flame. Batata poha is ready for serving.
Sabudana Khichdi
3/4 cup small pearl Sabudana,2 Green Chillies, 5-7 Curry Leaves,2 medium Potatoes, 1/3 cup Roasted Peanuts,1/4 teaspoon Turmeric Powder, 2 teaspoons Fresh Lemon Juice,2 teaspoons Sugar,Salt,2 tablespoons Cooking Oil.
Preparetion
1.Wash and soak 3/4 cup sabudana in 1/2 cup water for 2 hours.After 2 hours, you will notice that its size has increased to almost double.
2.Drain excess water, if any, and transfer it to a colander or a plate. Let it stand for 2 hours. If you do not let it stand for some time, then they may turn sticky while cooking.
3. Cut the green chilli and curry leaves and boiled potatoes, crushed peanuts,lemon juice, sugar and garam masala powder.
How to Make sabudana Khichdi
*Heat oil or ghee over medium flame in a heavy based aluminum or non-stick pan.Add cumin seeds in heated oil.and add green chilli and curry leaves and sauté for 10 seconds.
*Add drained sabudana and mix thoroughly. Cook for 2-3 minutes while stirring continuously.Add turmeric powder and salt, mix well.
*Cook for 7-8 minutes over medium flame until sabudana turns translucent.Add boiled potatoes, coarsely crushed peanuts, grated coconut, lemon juice, sugar and garam masala powder.
*Mix well and cook for 3 minutes,Turn off the flame. Transfer khichdi to a serving bowl and garnish with coriander leaves.
Veg-Biryani
2 tablespoons oil, onion, ½ teaspoon cumin seed, 1 tablespoon garlic paste, 1 tomato, potato, carrot, salt, red chile pepper,black pepper, ½ teaspoon garam masala,turmeric, 2 cups basmati rice.
How to Make Veg-Biryani
1.Melt oil in a large Dutch oven over medium heat. Add onion, and cook until softened, about 3 minutes. Stir in cumin seed, cinnamon stick, and peppercorns; cook until the spices are fragrant, and the cumin seeds begin to pop, about 3 minutes.
2.Stir in ginger garlic paste, tomatoes, and 1/2 cup water. Bring to a simmer, and cook until the water has evaporated, about 5 minutes. Stir in peas, carrot, and potato. Season with chicken bouillon, salt, red chile, black pepper, garam masala, and turmeric. Stir well, then cover, and cook for 3 minutes.
3.Pour in 4 cups water and bring to a boil over high heat. Once boiling, stir in basmati rice, reduce heat to medium, recover, and simmer for 10 minutes. Reduce heat to low and continue to cook until the rice has softened, 10 to 15 minutes more.
Curd Rice
2cups cooked rice,2cups curds, 1cup milk, 10curry leaves, 2red chilies, 1teaspoon cumin seed, 2tablespoons oil, 1tablespoon chana dal, salt, sugar,coriander leaves.
How to Make Curd Rice
1.Take rice in a pressure cooker, add in 4 cups of water, cook it for 5 whistle. Simmer for 5 mins and turn off the stove, let the pressure go all by itself. Open the cooker and mash it up with a spoon. Let the rice get to room temp.
2.Now add in salt, curd and milk to the rice and mix well. Add in cucumber, carrot and green chilli and mix well.Heat oil in a small pan. Add in tempering ingredients. Mustard seeds, split urad dal, curry leaves. You can add green chillies in tempering.Let the spices sizzle in the oil for a full minute for the flavours to develop and gets toasty.
3.Now make tempering by heating oil and crackling all the spices and ginger. Let them get golden. Pour this over the rice and mix well. Add in coriander leaves and mix well. Put it in the fridge for 1 hour. Serve with pickle.
